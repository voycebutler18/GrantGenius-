Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI grant assistant work? +

Our AI assistant analyzes your project details and generates professional grant proposal sections tailored to your specific needs. Simply describe your project, and our AI will help you create compelling narratives, budget justifications, and evaluation plans.

What types of grants can you help with? +

We support all types of grants including nonprofit, educational, health, environmental, research, and community development grants. Our AI is trained on diverse grant requirements and can adapt to various funding sources.

Is my information secure? +

Yes, we take data security seriously. All information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your project details with third parties. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How accurate are the AI-generated proposals? +

Our AI generates high-quality, professional content based on best practices and successful grant proposals. However, we recommend reviewing and customizing the output to match your specific requirements and funder guidelines.
