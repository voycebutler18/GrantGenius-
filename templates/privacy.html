Grant Genius Hub Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Last Updated: July 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

Personal Information

Information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, organization details, and project descriptions when using our grant assistance services.

  • Account Information: Name, email address, organization name, and contact details
  • Project Data: Grant proposal content, project descriptions, and related documentation
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our services, including pages visited and features used
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access times

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and improve our AI-powered grant writing services
  • Generate personalized grant proposal content and recommendations
  • Communicate with you about your account and services
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
