Privacy Policy & Data Security

Comprehensive protection for your sensitive grant information

Information Collection and Use

Grant Genius Hub collects and processes information you provide when creating grant proposals, including project descriptions, organizational details, budget information, target demographics, and other relevant data necessary to generate comprehensive grant applications. We use this information exclusively to provide our AI-powered grant writing services, improve our platform's effectiveness, enhance user experience, and ensure the quality of generated proposals meets professional standards required by funding organizations.

Data Security and Protection Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data transmission protocols, multi-factor authentication, and strict access controls to protect your sensitive information. All data is processed through secure, SOC 2 compliant servers with regular security audits and penetration testing. We maintain detailed logs of all data access and modifications, ensuring complete transparency and accountability in our data handling practices.

Grant Writing Process and Confidentiality

Our AI-powered grant writing process analyzes your input to generate tailored proposals, budget justifications, evaluation plans, sustainability frameworks, and other essential grant components. We maintain absolute confidentiality regarding your project details, funding requirements, strategic plans, and organizational information. Your grant proposals, research data, and related materials are never shared with third parties, competitors, or other users without explicit written consent, ensuring your competitive advantage throughout the funding process.

User Rights and Data Management

You maintain complete control over your data with rights to access, update, export, or delete your personal information and grant-related materials at any time. We provide comprehensive tools within our platform to manage your data preferences, download your content, and request complete data removal. Our retention policies ensure that information is kept only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted using DoD-approved methods.

Contact Information and Support

For questions about this privacy policy, data handling practices, security measures, or to exercise your privacy rights, contact our dedicated support team at info@grantgeniushub.com or call 720-306-1898. Our privacy team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST, and is committed to addressing all privacy concerns promptly and transparently, ensuring you feel confident about how your sensitive information is handled throughout the grant writing process.
